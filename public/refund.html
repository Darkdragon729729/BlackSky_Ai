Refund & Cancellation Policy

Effective Date: July 3, 2026

1. Digital Services

Our website provides digital AI services only. Once wallet balance is successfully credited after payment, the transaction is considered complete.

2. Refund Eligibility

Wallet recharges are generally non-refundable after they have been successfully credited. If your payment is successful but your wallet is not credited due to a technical issue, please contact our support team.

3. Failed or Duplicate Payments

If your payment fails but money is deducted, or if you are charged more than once for the same transaction, please contact us with your Razorpay Payment ID. We will investigate the issue and take appropriate action.

4. Cancellation

Because our services are delivered digitally, completed wallet recharges cannot normally be cancelled after successful processing.

5. Processing Time

If a refund is approved according to our policy, it will be processed using the original payment method. Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

6. Contact Support

For refund or payment-related questions, please contact us through the Contact page and include your Razorpay Payment ID and transaction details.
